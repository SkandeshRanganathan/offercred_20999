About Us


OfferCred is a platform designed to verify and showcase authentic student opportunities. Students struggle to prove achievements, and recruiters waste time validating claims.

We Empower Students & Recruiters By:

  • Verifying companies via government portals and domain checks
  • Validating certificates using advanced RSM technology
  • Creating trusted digital profiles with verified achievements
  • Providing data-backed reviews on courses and internships

Our Mission

Eliminate fake claims, reduce skill gaps, promote genuine talent, and create a transparent system where real achievements are recognized and valued by both students and recruiters.

Services

Empowering students and recruiters with verified opportunities and credibility insights

Certificate Verification Service

Students upload internship, job offer, or course certificates. OfferCred verifies authenticity via MCA/Startup India checks, domain validation, and Record Similarity Matching (RSM). Verified certificates are stored in the student’s profile.

Credibility Insights & Analytics

Provides insights on companies and courses through peer reviews, course completion stats, and company credibility reports—helping students make informed decisions.

Student Profile Building

Build a credibility-first profile showing only verified achievements. Acts as a digital portfolio of trust for internships, courses, and projects.

Recruiter Verification Service

Recruiters can verify certificates via PDF/photo uploads or manual entry. Fast-track verification is available if a student’s verified profile exists.

Preventing Fraud & Misrepresentation

Detects and flags fake certificates, non-existent companies, and unverifiable domains, promoting accountability and reducing false clout in student communities.

Trust Network & Community Reviews

Aggregates student experiences of companies and courses. Provides transparent analytics, reputation scores, ratings, and feedback for students, recruiters, and companies.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is OfferCred?

OfferCred is a platform that verifies student opportunities like internships, job offers, and course certificates, helping students showcase genuine achievements and enabling recruiters to hire real, verified talent.

How does OfferCred verify certificates and companies?

We verify companies using government databases like MCA India and Startup India, plus domain checks. Certificates are validated using advanced Recognition & Similarity Matching (RSM) technology to ensure authenticity.

How does OfferCred benefit students?

Students build trusted digital profiles with verified certificates and experiences, access real reviews on courses and companies, and make informed decisions to grow their careers without wasting time or money.

How does OfferCred help recruiters?

Recruiters can instantly access verified candidate profiles, quickly confirm certificates and company credibility, and make faster, more confident hiring decisions while avoiding fake claims.

Is OfferCred useful for course selection?

Yes! OfferCred provides data-driven analytics and real student reviews on courses and companies, helping students choose only credible learning opportunities for real skill development.

What is the mission of OfferCred?

Our mission is to eliminate fake claims, reduce skill gaps, and create a transparent ecosystem where real talent is recognized and rewarded by both students and recruiters.
